### Evaluation Metrics for Generative Pedestrian Crossing Models in Simulation

Working with [Golam Md. Muktadir (UC Santa Cruz)](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=RDa4TOgAAAAJ&hl=en), [Dr. A.B.M. Alim Al Islam (BUET)](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=K-AIPzQAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Dr. Mahmuda Naznin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=dJx58AQAAAAJ&hl=en).

Analysis of real-life pedestrian behavior data from datasets in order to formulate
metrics to compare and evaluate generative pedestrian models in autonomous
vehicle simulations. Data analysis, visualization, and statistical modeling are the primary tools being used in this research.
