Working under [Dr. Golam Md. Muktadir](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=RDa4TOgAAAAJ&hl=en), on a project to analyze the thinking capability in large language models (LLMs) and testing the thinking capability in LLM for solving difficult problems in absurd language. This project aims to find out if the reasoning models do actually "think" about a problem, or provide the solutions.
