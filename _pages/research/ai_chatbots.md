Working under the supervision of [Dr. Dipto Das](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=yRHRA4QAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Dr. Imtiaz Karim](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YqQRjfgAAAAJ&hl=en), this project entails the collection and analysis of anonymous user reviews in online communities to understand and imply specific privacy concerns relevant to romantic or emotional AI chatbots.
